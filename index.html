About me

A lifelong passion.

Born in 1994, my love for photography began very early. I was just six years old when I started playing with my parents' camera, going through roll after roll of film with the curiosity and enthusiasm typical of childhood. That early passion convinced my parents it was more than just a game — soon came a photography book and my very first camera of my own.

For my First Communion, I received my first digital camera, a true technological revolution at the time. Despite this, for many years I continued to prefer film, fascinated by its process and its magic.

At eighteen, I bought my first DSLR with gift money — a camera that marked a turning point in my journey. It also gave me the opportunity to collaborate with local newspapers, taking my first steps into the professional world of photography.

Born and raised in the province of Naples, I have been living in Geneva for almost a decade, where I moved for work. An engineer by profession, married, photography has always been a constant presence in my life — the passion that has burned longest inside me. Like all authentic passions, it has gone through periods of pause, but each time it has come back stronger than before.
